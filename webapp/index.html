One stroke, one word. Named for Thelonious Monk — fewer notes, more music.

How it works

Instead of typing a p p l e one key at a time, press a+p+l together and hit space. Monk infers the word — apple — from the chord and the context. When two words fit the same chord, a bar appears: pick with 1–9, ←/→, or space.
