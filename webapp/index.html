How it works

Instead of typing a p p l e one key at a time, press a+p+l together and hit space. Comp infers the word — apple — from the chord and the context. When two words fit the same chord, a bar appears: pick with 1–9, ←/→, or space.

Pick a language, then work through the sets. Three stars each unlocks real speed.

Rule of thumb for a chord: first letter + a strong middle consonant + last letter. Type normally any time — full words always pass straight through.
